Strategy, Planning, and Performance Management Department

Our Department

Strategy, Planning & Performance Management is charged with ensuring that the School achieves and sustains superior performance by linking tangible actions to a clear corporate vision. We work together with directorates to develop a strategic plan that is robust, flexible and easily implemented within their organizational context.

In Order to support strategic decision-making and operational management processes that are carried out in full compliance with the mandates of the School and to improve processes that increase efficiency and effectiveness, this unit conducts regular monitoring and evaluation. We perform analyses to ensure greater transparency and accountability.

The monitoring and evaluation activities are constantly evolving, building on best practices and new developments in technology to provide accurate and up-to-date information on performance. Our functions go hand in hand with knowledge management, the standardization of operating procedures and innovation across all the School.

1. What we do

Oversee the development, monitoring and review of the School’s Strategic planning and implementation process to meet the School’s mandate.

2. What we do

Advice management and oversee the development and implementation of the School’s quality management system to maintain quality standards in the School.

3. What we do

Ensure adherence to governance obligations, and statutory and regulatory requirements to ensure compliance with legal obligations.

4. What we do

Develop or coordinate and manage implementation of identified innovations to enhance competitiveness and sustainability.

5. What we do

Oversee the development and implementation of an action plan based on corporate risk management framework to ensure business continuity in the School.

6. What we do

Guide the annual target setting process leading to performance contract signing and cascading the performance contracts in line with relevant statutory guidelines to realize the strategic plan objectives

Performance Contracting & Principles

Performance Contracting

Performance Contract is a tool aimed at improving efficiency and effectiveness in the management of public service.

It involves four main stages;

1. Negotiation,

2. Vetting,

3. Implementation, and

4. Evaluation.

Performance Contracting Principles

The following are the Performance Contracting Principles;

1. What gets measured gets done,

2. If you don’t measure results, you cant tell success from failure,

3. If you can’t see success, you cant reward it

4. If you cant see success, you cant learn from it,

5. If you cant recognize failure, you cant correct it,

6. If you cant demonstrate results, you cant win public support
